Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our support line directly for immediate assistance.

Can I schedule a free consultation?

Yes! We offer free 15-minute consultations for new students. You can schedule one through our online booking system or by calling our office. This is a great way to discuss your learning goals and see if our tutoring services are right for you.

How do I request a specific tutor?

You can request a specific tutor by mentioning their name in the message field of this contact form, or by specifying your preference when you sign up for a tutoring plan. We'll do our best to accommodate your request based on the tutor's availability.

Can I visit your center before signing up?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective students to visit our learning center. Please contact us to schedule a tour, and one of our staff members will be happy to show you around and answer any questions you might have.
